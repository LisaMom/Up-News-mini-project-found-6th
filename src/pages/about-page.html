Who we are

A newsroom, not a feed.

We started as a small team of reporters convinced that speed shouldn't come at the cost of accuracy. Every story runs through a fact-check pass before it reaches a reader.

Every story runs through a real editor before it goes live. We enforce a strict correction policy, name our sources whenever we can, and keep opinion out of the reporting pages.
